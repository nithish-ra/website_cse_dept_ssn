B.E. Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 1996. The undergraduate program B.E (CSE) has been accredited by NBA. The department offers state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, Wi-Fi enabled classrooms with laptops and overhead projectors to facilitate e-Learning, research laboratories with high-speed computing machines, a well-equipped seminar hall, a fully equipped conference hall, and a well-stocked department library for the holistic development of students.

Curriculum

The department offers 25 professional core subjects, 39 professional elective subjects, 8 open elective subjects oriented towards three specialization areas: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Software Engineering & Systems, and Security. In addition, it includes 4 employability enhancement courses, 2 mandatory courses, and 9 engineering sciences subjects.
