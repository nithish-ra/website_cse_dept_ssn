Research Work

The Department of CSE is a recognized research center under Anna University to pursue Ph.D. and MS (Research) programs. More than 90% of the faculty are Ph.D. holders, most of whom are recognized research supervisors at Anna University.

Collaborative Research

The department actively collaborates with institutions and industries worldwide including University of Cyprus, Kettering University (USA), University of Doha, IIITDM Chennai, IIIT Una, Caterpillar, Valeo, HCL, Barclays, Geeplex Pvt. Ltd. (Singapore), Mikanko, ACI, Senquire, Regenboog India Foundation, Tyquee Solutions, and Ford. Funded projects have been supported by DST, BIRAC, AICTE, ISRO, and TNSCST.

Curriculum

The five-year integrated M.Tech program follows the Outcome Based Education (OBE) paradigm, ensuring well-defined Programme Outcomes (PO) and Programme Educational Objectives (PEO). It includes theory, theory-cum-practical, and practical courses along with humanities, social sciences, basic sciences, engineering sciences, professional core and elective courses, open electives, online courses (NPTEL/SWAYAM), mandatory courses, and audit courses.

Specialized courses include Business Analytics, Industrial Safety, Operations Research, Cost Management of Research Projects, Waste-to-Energy, and Composite Materials. Students may also opt for value-added courses conducted by industry experts and research faculty.
